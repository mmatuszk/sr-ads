# Pinterest Budget Change

Date: 2026-04-23

## Scope

This note records the Pinterest budget change discussed after the cross-channel performance review.

## Change

- budget: `145 -> 115`
  - change: `-30`
  - percent change: `-20.7%`

## Rationale

This change was made to reduce spend moderately while preserving enough activity to:

- keep the campaign in market
- avoid an unnecessarily hard reset in platform learning
- detect demand recovery earlier than if the campaign were cut too aggressively

## Operating note

Recommended review window after the change:

- allow at least `7-14 days` before another material budget change unless performance deteriorates sharply
