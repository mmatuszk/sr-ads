# Cross-Channel Demand Review

Date: 2026-04-23

## Scope

This note records the cross-channel business trend review covering:

- Shopify / Google Analytics
- Google Ads
- Pinterest
- eBay
- Etsy
- broader demand context

## Main conclusion

The recent weakness appears to be broad-based and is unlikely to be explained mainly by a Google tracking issue.

The evidence is more consistent with:

- softer demand
- lower conversion efficiency
- weaker traffic quality in some paid channels

## Shopify / Google Analytics

Comparison reviewed:

- `2026-03-26` to `2026-04-22`
- versus `2026-02-26` to `2026-03-25`

Observed changes:

- total revenue: `$39,627.20 -> $19,826.86` (`-49.97%`)
- key events: `156 -> 127` (`-18.59%`)
- session key event rate: `0.71% -> 0.52%` (`-26.14%`)

Cross-network row:

- sessions: `8,504 -> 8,605` (`+1.19%`)
- revenue: `$15,462.29 -> $7,226.10` (`-53.27%`)

Interpretation:

- traffic volume was not the main issue
- monetization and conversion efficiency weakened sharply

Additional note:

- GA showed a direct-session anomaly around `2026-04-18`
- this makes top-line traffic noisier than the revenue signal

## Pinterest

Visible comparison:

- last `30` days versus previous `30` days

Observed changes:

- spend: `+14%`
- impressions: `+3%`
- checkouts: `-17%`

Interpretation:

- Pinterest reach held up
- checkout outcomes weakened

## eBay

Observed metrics from Seller Hub:

- last `31` days sales: `$821.48`
- last `90` days sales: `$3,678.57`

Implied `30-day` average from the `90-day` total:

- `$3,678.57 / 3 = about $1,226.19`

Comparison:

- current recent period versus 90-day average: about `-33%`

Supporting eBay signals:

- listing impressions: `-23.9%`
- listing page views: `+16.6%`
- sales conversion rate: down by `0.1` percentage points

Interpretation:

- eBay also showed meaningful weakness
- lower conversion efficiency was part of the pattern

## Etsy

Recent period reviewed:

- last `30` days: `2026-03-25` to `2026-04-23`
- visits: `4,009`
- orders: `7`
- revenue: `$2,264`

Broader baseline reviewed:

- custom range: `2026-01-24` to `2026-04-23`
- visits: `16.6K`
- orders: `29`
- revenue: `$10,088`

Implied `30-day` average from that broader baseline:

- visits: about `5,533`
- orders: about `9.7`
- revenue: about `$3,363`

Comparison:

- visits: about `-28%`
- orders: about `-28%`
- revenue: about `-33%`

Interpretation:

- Etsy is low volume and noisier
- even after allowing for noise, the recent period was below its broader recent pace

## Macro / category context

Macro context reviewed during the investigation suggested:

- some softness in home / furnishings demand
- cautious consumer behavior
- no evidence of a broad ecommerce collapse severe enough to explain all recent weakness by itself

Interpretation:

- macro softness may be contributing
- it does not appear sufficient on its own to explain the full decline

## Final interpretation

Taken together, the most likely explanation is:

- broader demand softness with weaker recent conversion efficiency

Less likely explanations:

- Google conversion tracking failure
- Pinterest setup failure
- a single-platform technical issue
