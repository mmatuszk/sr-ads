# Google Ads Budget Changes

Date: 2026-04-23

## Scope

This note records Google Ads budget changes discussed after the cross-channel performance review.

## Changes

- `Max-2`: `360 -> 285`
  - change: `-75`
  - percent change: `-20.8%`
- `Max-13`: `60 -> 45`
  - change: `-15`
  - percent change: `-25.0%`

## Rationale

These changes were made in response to weaker recent performance and broader demand softness.

Working assumptions at the time of the decision:

- Google performance had weakened materially
- the weakness did not appear to be caused by broken conversion tracking
- market weakness was showing across multiple channels, not only Google Ads
- budgets should be reduced moderately rather than cut aggressively, to preserve learning and maintain visibility if demand starts recovering

## Operating note

Recommended review window after the change:

- hold for `7-14 days` before making another material budget change unless performance deteriorates sharply
